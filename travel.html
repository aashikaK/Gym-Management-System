DESTINATIONS

Immerse yourself in Kathmandu Valley's allure by exploring Swayambhunath, Durbar Squares of Kathmandu, Patan, and Bhaktapur, venerating at Pashupatinath and Boudhanath, and relishing the ancient charm of Changunarayan, all encapsulating Nepal's rich cultural and historical heritage.

Embark on a spiritual journey in Lumbini, tracing the footsteps of Buddha at the Sacred Garden, Maya Devi Temple, and Ashoka Pillar, while also exploring the Lumbini Museum and various monastic zones that narrate the profound legacy of Buddhism.

Envelop yourself in Pokhara's natural beauty by boating on Phewa Lake, marveling at the ethereal Peace Pagoda, witnessing the cascading Devi's Fall, and hiking up to Sarangkot's viewpoint for panoramic Himalayan vistas, creating an unforgettable Himalayan escapade.

Nepal's national parks offer diverse ecosystems and wildlife. From Chitwan's rhinos and tigers to Sagarmatha's alpine majesty, each park, including Langtang, Bardia, and Rara, presents unique opportunities for nature enthusiasts to explore and appreciate the country's rich biodiversity

Embrace the awe-inspiring journey to Sagarmatha National Park, home to the world's highest peak, Mount Everest. Trek through picturesque Sherpa villages and rugged landscapes for an unforgettable Himalayan adventure.

Nepal's rich cultural diversity blooms in its various ethnicities, such as Newars, Gurungs, and Tharus, each contributing unique traditions and festivals. From urban centers like Kathmandu to remote villages, experiencing Nepal's varied cultural tapestry is a captivating journey.
